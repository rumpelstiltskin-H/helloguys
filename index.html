About St. Ann’s Spot Reserve

St. Ann’s Spot Reserve is your one-stop solution for booking halls at St. Ann’s College. It replaces manual processes with an online booking system, intuitive platform, offering real-time venue availability. Key features include an approval workflow, conflict management with alternative suggestions, and a centralized dashboard for administrators to monitor usage and generate reports. Whether it’s a seminar, workshop, or event, we’ve got you covered!
